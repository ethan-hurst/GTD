  <how-to-verify>
Test the complete onboarding flow:

1. **Fresh start:** Clear browser data for localhost (DevTools > Application > Clear Site Data). Reload the app.
2. **Walkthrough wizard:** Verify full-screen wizard appears with "Welcome to GTD" heading.
3. **Skip option:** Verify "I know GTD" skip button appears on welcome screen only.
4. **Capture step:** Click "Let's go", verify Capture step shows. Type a task (e.g., "Test task"), press Enter. Verify confetti fires, task is captured.
5. **Process step:** Advance to Process step. Verify GTD decision tree is displayed clearly.
6. **Organize step:** Advance to Organize step. Verify 4 GTD list cards are shown (Next Actions, Projects, Waiting For, Someday/Maybe).
7. **Review step:** Advance to Review step. Click "Get Started". Verify confetti fires and wizard closes.
8. **Post-walkthrough:** Verify app is now visible with sidebar. Check that sidebar links show hint indicators (blue dots or similar) on unvisited features.
9. **Hint content:** Hover/click a hint on "Next Actions" link. Verify tooltip shows bold title + GTD explanation.
10. **Hint dismissal:** Navigate to /actions. Return to sidebar. Verify "Next Actions" hint is gone (feature was visited).
11. **Persistence:** Refresh the page. Verify wizard does NOT reappear. Verify visited feature hints stay dismissed.
12. **Settings reset:** Go to Settings. Click "Reset Onboarding". Confirm dialog. Refresh page. Verify wizard reappears.
13. **Skip flow:** On wizard reappearance, click "I know GTD". Verify wizard closes immediately. Navigate to a feature. Verify hints are shorter/UI-focused (not full GTD explanations).
14. **Dark mode:** Toggle dark mode. Verify wizard and hints render correctly in both themes.
15. **Inbox verification:** Go to Inbox. Verify the task captured during walkthrough ("Test task") appears in the inbox list.
