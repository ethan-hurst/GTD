## Decisions Made

- Sync itemCount retrieved from db.items.count() after sync completes (performSync doesn't return count)
- All analytics calls placed after primary action completes to ensure non-blocking, fail-safe pattern
- Task.created fires when items move from inbox to any category (not just final completion)
